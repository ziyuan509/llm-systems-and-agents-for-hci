# Research Questions

## Primary Question

How can ordinary users govern delegation to long-running research agents without relying on prompt engineering or developer-style debugging views?

## Working Research Questions

- RQ1: What policy objects are understandable and actionable for end users when configuring a deep research agent?
- RQ2: How should an agent decide when to act autonomously versus ask back under a user-defined delegation contract?
- RQ3: Compared with black-box chat and trace-oriented oversight, does a governance-oriented interface improve correction quality, calibrated reliance, and perceived control?

## Non-Goals

- not a general-purpose multi-agent debugger
- not a prompt engineering workbench
- not a generic privacy dashboard detached from task execution
