# Research Questions

## Main Question

How can ordinary users define, revise, and repair delegation boundaries for
privacy-sensitive pervasive AI agents?

## Working Research Questions

- RQ1: What sensing, memory, action, disclosure, and escalation policy objects are understandable and actionable for ordinary users?
- RQ2: How should a context-aware AI agent decide when to act autonomously versus ask back under a user-defined delegation contract?
- RQ3: How can repair after a privacy boundary conflict update future agent behavior without requiring users to rewrite prompts?
- RQ4: Compared with black-box and oversight-only baselines, does a delegation-contract interface improve perceived control, calibrated reliance, and accountability?

## Non-Goals

- not a general-purpose multi-agent debugger
- not a prompt engineering workbench
- not a generic privacy dashboard detached from agent action
- not a production ambient sensing system
